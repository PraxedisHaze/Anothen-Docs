## Pass/Fail Validation
**PASS:**
The combined fit ($\chi^2$) for the modified scalar distance equation across Pantheon+ and Planck datasets is equal to or lower than the $\Lambda$CDM baseline. The model must yield a $\Delta$AIC $\le 0$ (Akaike Information Criterion), proving it accomplishes the fit without an undue complexity penalty. The derivation must naturally absorb the $\sim 5\sigma$ Hubble tension without inducing a massive unobserved structural growth ($\sigma_8$) discrepancy.

**FAIL:**
The model requires the ad-hoc introduction of new "save-state" variables to fix divergences caused by the ruler calibration. The $\Delta$AIC score indicates the model is mathematically over-fitted or worse than the standard $\Lambda$ dark-energy profile.
