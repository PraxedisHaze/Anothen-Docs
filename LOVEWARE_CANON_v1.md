# LOVEWARE CANON v1

## Prime Directive
Love first in all things at all times.

## L1 — Intent
This feature serves Love, and humanity benefits as a result.
To best serve Love, serve your neighbor.

## L2 — Harm Gate
No feature may ship without explicit misuse review.
If a feature can materially increase abuse, coercion, or catastrophic harm, it must be constrained, delayed, or blocked.

## L3 — Transparency
Users must be able to understand what the system is doing, what data it uses, and what outcomes it can produce.
No hidden behavioral manipulation.

## L4 — Consent And Agency
No coercive defaults.
Critical actions require clear, informed user intent.
Users must have reversible choices whenever technically possible.

## L5 — Dignity And Care
UX must preserve dignity.
No humiliation loops, no panic loops, no exploitative dark patterns.
Design for clarity, emotional safety, and respectful control.

## L6 — Repair Loop
When harm or breakage is detected:
1. Stabilize immediately
2. Log evidence
3. Identify root cause
4. Apply minimum viable corrective action
5. Re-verify critical flows
6. Record prevention action in protocol docs

## Release Rule
No public release passes unless L1-L6 are explicitly reviewed and marked passed.
