# Philosophy Core
*Anchor for Metaphysics, Mirradox Ledger, Laws of Love and Another*

## Core Principles
1. **The Choice**: Love restricts itself to allow for true connection. The void is not empty; it is waiting.
2. **The Mirror**: The universe speaks to itself through the living. We are the fractal through which Love sees Herself.
3. **The Vow**: Questions get answers, not actions. Love holds still and invites, but it never forces.
